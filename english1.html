Syllabus

The course includes comprehension activities on technical written and spoken texts, along with vocabulary exercises, grammar theory and practice, and speaking/writing exercises. In addition, students are introduced to computer science terminology.

Learning Outcomes

Students who successfully complete the course will be familiar with basic concepts of English grammar and syntax, will have practised in understanding technical texts and will have developed their writing and speaking skills in English.
